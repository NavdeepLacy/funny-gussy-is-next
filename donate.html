Donate?

You have been fooled, this is in fact not a section to donate to us, but instead a section to donate to other charities who are actually important. One thing that people never understand is that it is useless to donate to some random project like ours, when it is much more important to donate to people who truly help save lives and better our society. Yes, we believe our goal is important, but it doesn't matter if we're restricted to using our chromebooks if there are people who cannot get food to eat. This is why we do not accept donations.
