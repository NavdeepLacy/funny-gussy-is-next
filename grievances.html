List of Grievances

Here we have an official list of grievances, or issues that we have with GCPS as of recently. This mostly involves the removal of the wifi network, but recent actions have also uncovered more concerning information that we wish to discuss.

GCPS has removed our wifi, causing safety issues, inconveniencing students, and jeopardizing our education.

Clubs cannot use school wifi to schedule meetings, nor can students bring their own devices in. However, the most egregious issue that has risen due to this decision is a safety one. School shootings, while rarer than people think, still happen. If I'm going to die, you know damn well that I'm going to want to send a message to my parents, and if the county wants to stop that from happening, they are truly insane. Only a few people ever abuse the wifi, and it's clearly an overreaction to remove it and punish the many because of the few. We're fine with compromises, like stricter rules for phone usage, but we're not okay with being treated like little children just because a few of our peers act like they are.

GCPS has spied on us repeatedly, often without us knowing, or without explicit consent

GCPS employs extremely invasive spyware methods on their devices that they have forced us to use. Generally this issue wouldn't be as huge of a problem if we had a choice to bring our own devices, but we don't. Given how security is often not great at public schools, the fact that the school knows our browsing habits better than we do is concerning. We demand more transparency and only necessary tracking on students instead of these invasive, unnecessary methods.

GCPS Employs a wifi filter that does not protect the school, and only hinders our education

The wifi filters used by GCPS often block critical websites that pose little to no threat to the school. Things like discord should be okay for use during school time, just not in classrooms, and the fact that this isn't possible is rediculous. I want to message my parents using signal and if that is such a problem to the county, it says a lot about what their priorities are as a government body that is supposed to educate children. Not to mention that unecessary censorship in a place of learning just undermines the environment supposed to be fostered there.

Closing statement

Therefore, we, the FUNGUS team, and students of Gwinnett County, demand that GCPS immediately reinstate the BYOD network, modify the network filter to be more effective, warn people of tracking, and give a definitive list of information collected by GCPS.

GCPS can either understand our motives and goals, and revoke these ill-concieved decisions, or they can fight an uphill battle and continually be outsmarted by a group of high-schoolers doing this in their free time.
