Accessing Wifi in School

Wanna watch YouTube without being restricted by the County? Want to access Duckduckgo, Discord, or Signal to do a multitude of useful things that the County doesn't want you to do, like message your parents or friends, without using your precious data? Well, we at F.U.N.G.U.S. have a solution.

As you know, the days of being able to use GCPS-Mobile at many schools in Gwinnett County are long gone. The county has disabled the ability to access GCPS-Mobile without authentication in each school that has chosen to disable BYOD. To make things worse, GCPS-Mobile is almost impenetrable, as each computer has a special configuration for access on the network, and it is difficult to reverse engineer.

However, there is still hope, GCPS-Guest still exists, and the school admins have not done anything to remove it, so it's still available.

Accessing the Guest Network

Add a hidden network called 'GCPS-Guest' (case-sensitive). Usually you can do this in the WiFi settings menu of your device, by selecting an option to 'enter Wifi SSID Manually' or some such. We don't have the time - or, frankly, the energy - to provide you with a guide to it for each individual device, so that's all we'll give you. I'm confident you can figure that out.

Getting to the sign-in page

After you add the network, it will usually say 'network requires you to sign in' or some such, and you'll have to find out how to get to the sign-in page. If you can't find it, use this link here.

Creating an Account

Once you are on the login page, click "create account." Here you can create an account with an E-Mail (you can also use a phone number, but it's a really bad idea, and not required). We highly reccomend mail services like this one that give you a temp email address and are verified to work. The school admins do periodically check the wifi network and block emails from logging in, so it is best to use temporary email services. Plus, this ensures your anonymity, but for the first time you can always use your own email if it doesn't have personally identifying information.

Obtaining the password

You will receive a password through the E-Mail you use, and once you use that password you should be able to log in. However, be sure to try again a few times if it does not work (because everything used by GCPS doesn't work half the time) and maybe try creating a new account if you're still stuck. Once you are connected, you can use your device - whether it be a PC, MAC, or even a Nintendo Switch - with ease.

Circumventing Restrictions

Do note that typical school restrictions do apply, you can check our guide on VPNs if you want more information. Otherwise, congrats! You now have internet.
